About Dead Pixel Detector

**Dead Pixel Detector** was born from a simple necessity: to offer a screen diagnostic tool that is **fast, free, and 100% reliable** without requiring installations or compromising user privacy. In a market full of heavy software and expensive monitors, we believe that checking the quality of a panel should be a simple and accessible process for everyone.

1. Our Mission and Philosophy

Our mission is to empower users by giving them full control over the health of their display. We specialize in the precise detection of visual flaws, helping to identify that tiny **dead pixel** or **stuck pixel** early, which could be a sign of a larger issue or a reason to claim warranty.

  • **Accessibility:** The tool works directly in your browser (100% Web).
  • **Privacy:** We do not use tracking cookies and do not collect personally identifiable information.
  • **Dual Diagnosis:** We offer both the detector (color tests) and the potential **pixel repairer** via software.

2. Why Trust Us with Your Screen?

Unlike other solutions, **Dead Pixel Detector** focuses exclusively on functionality and safety. By not requiring proprietary software or complex access, we eliminate any risk of malware installation or system instability. The integrity of your hardware is our priority, which is why the tool remains simple and focused on its goal: visual quality testing.
