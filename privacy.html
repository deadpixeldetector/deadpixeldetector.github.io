Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 23, 2025

Welcome to Dead Pixel Detector. Your privacy is our priority. This policy details our commitment to **not using tracking cookies** or collecting personal information for tracking or personalized advertising purposes.

1. Information We Collect

Dead Pixel Detector does not directly collect Personally Identifiable Information (PII). The tool runs entirely in your browser without the need for servers for data processing.

  • Minimal Usage Data: We may use Google Analytics to collect aggregated, anonymous usage data (pages visited, time spent, etc.) without using persistent cookies that allow individual tracking.
  • Site Preferences: Your language preference (Spanish/English) is temporarily stored only for your current session, without the use of persistent cookies.

2. Our Commitment to Not Using Cookies

The Website is designed to function **without the use of cookies for tracking, analytics, or targeted advertising.**

If, for any technical reason (e.g., basic hosting operation), cookies are used, they are strictly technical and essential for functionality, not for user tracking.

3. Third-Party Services (Non-Personalized Ads)

We monetize the website through **Google AdSense**. To honor your preference for not using cookies, Google AdSense is configured to display **Non-Personalized Ads (NPA)**.

  • Non-Personalized Ads (NPA): These ads are targeted solely based on the site's content (monitor tests, technology, gaming) and general geographical information, without relying on cookies for your user profile or browsing history.
  • **Google Analytics (if used):** This service is configured with the IP anonymization option and with the strictest settings to prevent user profiling.
  • You can review Google's policy on this type of advertising: About non-personalized ads.

4. Links and Security

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any other websites that may be accessed through links on our site.
